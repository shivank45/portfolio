My Journey

From teenage curiosity to building full-stack dreams

🔍 Exploring Deeper

🎓 College Life Begins

Now in my first year of college, I’m diving into C programming and sharpening my fundamentals. Every day is a new challenge, and I’m loving the grind.

🚀 Current Goals

I’m giving my best to learn full-stack development and aim to build a complete website from scratch—frontend to backend.

♟️ Beyond Code
